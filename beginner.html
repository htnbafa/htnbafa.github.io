The Complicated Explanation:

calories in has to be less than calories out

now let me dumb it down

you’re a pro eater, you know how to cram a bunch of tasty delicious food into your body and not use any of it
so the big secret everyone in the fitness industry doesn’t want you to know about is:
YOU HAVE TO EAT LESS how? by not putting as much food into your mouth.
pick from the following list of food you can eat less of (or completely eliminate from your diet):

  • those “healthy” low calorie snacks you eat every hour on the hour
  • the bowl of soup made of low-fat ranch dressing and croutons you call a salad
  • soft drinks
if completely cutting any of these items from your diet is too much for your fatass, try working up to it, just eating smaller amounts or eating less frequently
for example, instead of a granola bar every time you play with your phone, try only eating a granola bar every other time, then every other day, and so on
don’t worry, your body won’t go into starvation mode, scout’s honors
If you do this for a month and have not lost any weight, ask yourself this:
“have I added new snacks to my diet, drink more sodas, eat more ranch dressing to compensate for the smaller amounts I am eating of other items?
If not, you’re a god damn liar
