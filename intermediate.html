Blender Recommendentations

Buy either a Blendtec or a Vitamix, these are the only two brands of blenders I recommend. Although they are a bit more expensive than the average, definitely worth it. 7-8 year warranty and will “liquify” almost anything. If you can’t afford it and don’t mind having chunks in your smoothie, then do some market research on a decent blender in your price range. When you realize the hassle and time you waste blending multiple times, come back and pick one up. The cheapest model from either brand will do.

Blendtec is cheaper, Vitamix is nicer
